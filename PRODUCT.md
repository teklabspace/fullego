# Product

## Register

product

## Users

High-net-worth investors, their advisors, and platform admins. Investors browse
and manage luxury assets (yachts, jets, real estate, art, watches) alongside
financial portfolios; advisors service clients; admins run compliance and
approvals. The public marketplace is the shop window: prospective investors
arrive unauthenticated, evaluate listings, and convert to signup. Inside the
dashboard, the same people work through dense financial workflows (offers,
escrow, KYC, appraisals).

## Product Purpose

Akunuba is a luxury wealth & lifestyle management platform: one place to hold,
value, trade, and service both bankable assets and passion assets. Success on
the public side is a qualified signup started from a listing; success inside
the dashboard is a completed workflow (offer made, escrow funded, KYC passed)
without support contact.

## Brand Personality

Private-bank luxury: assured, discreet, precise. Three words: **opulent,
composed, trustworthy**. Near-black surfaces (#0B0D12 family) with a single
champagne-gold accent (#F1CB68) and generous whitespace. Feels like a members'
lounge, not a trading terminal.

## Anti-references

- Retail-broker density (Robinhood/eToro gamification, confetti, green/red
  everywhere).
- Crypto-site maximalism: neon gradients, glassmorphism stacks, animated
  charts as decoration.
- Generic SaaS marketing scaffolding: cream backgrounds, eyebrow labels over
  every section, identical icon-card grids.

## Design Principles

1. **One gold, spent sparingly** — the accent marks value and primary actions
   only; when everything glows gold, nothing is precious.
2. **The asset is the hero** — photography and the number (price/value) carry
   the page; chrome recedes.
3. **State is never ambiguous** — money and compliance flows always show a
   clear status, next step, and escape hatch.
4. **Quiet density** — wealth data is dense, but rhythm and hierarchy keep it
   calm; whitespace is part of the luxury.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

No formal WCAG mandate recorded; target WCAG AA pragmatically: ≥4.5:1 body
contrast on dark surfaces, visible focus states, reduced-motion alternatives
for all animation, semantic landmarks. Clientele skews older — body text never
below 14px effective.
